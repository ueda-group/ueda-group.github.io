# Ueda Group Website

Official website of the Ueda Group at the
Center for Quantum Information and Quantum Biology (QIQB),
The University of Osaka.

---

## Research Areas

The Ueda Group studies:

* Tensor network methods
* Quantum many-body physics
* Quantum algorithms
* Quantum-classical hybrid computation
* High-performance scientific computing (HPC)

A central theme of the group is the use of quantum entanglement as a computational principle connecting classical numerical methods and quantum computation.

Our research combines ideas from:

* Condensed matter physics
* Quantum information science
* Tensor-network representations
* Variational quantum algorithms
* Large-scale numerical simulation
* Quantum-HPC hybrid computation

The group aims to establish scalable computational methodologies for complex quantum systems by integrating tensor networks, quantum algorithms, scientific computing, and quantum hardware.
