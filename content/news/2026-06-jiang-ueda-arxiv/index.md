A new paper by Zhiyu Jiang and Hiroshi Ueda has been posted on arXiv.

Title:  
*"Isometrization of Tensor Network States via Gauge Propagation"*

The work introduces a gauge-propagation approach for approximately converting generic tensor-network states into an isometric tensor-network state form with a prescribed orthogonality center.

In one dimension, the propagation is exact, while in higher-dimensional tensor networks the method addresses local obstructions by approximating local tensors or contracted clusters using structured propagation-compatible decompositions.

The results identify gauge propagation as a useful building block for approximate isometrization and as a potential initializer or preconditioner for variational isometric tensor-network algorithms.
