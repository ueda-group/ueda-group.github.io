Hiroshi Ueda delivered an invited talk at
the 14th Workshop on Quantum Many-Body Computation
held in Beijing, China,
from April 10 to 12, 2026.

Title of the talk:

"Variational Quantum Partial SVD with Orthogonality Correction:
High-Precision Entanglement Spectra from Shallow Circuits"

The presentation introduced recent developments
in hybrid quantum-classical algorithms
for entanglement spectrum estimation,
variational quantum singular value decomposition,
and tensor-network-inspired quantum computation.

The workshop also provided opportunities
for discussions on quantum algorithms,
tensor networks,
and future international collaborations.
