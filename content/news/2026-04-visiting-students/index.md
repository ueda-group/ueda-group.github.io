We welcome several visiting students to the Ueda Group.

Current visiting students include members from:

- École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL)
- University of Toronto
- University of the Basque Country (UPV/EHU)

Their research covers tensor networks,
quantum many-body physics,
quantum simulation,
and quantum machine learning.

We look forward to fruitful international collaborations.
